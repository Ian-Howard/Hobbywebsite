About Hobby Club

Hobby Club is a secret society of hobby enthusiasts who come together to rediscover their true selves. We believe that only by confronting our fears and limitations can we truly grow as individuals.

Our hobbies are not about violence, but about achieving a state of mind that can only be reached through hobbying. Our members come from all walks of life, united in their quest for self-discovery and personal growth.

Rules

  1. You do not talk about Hobby Club.
  2. You DO NOT talk about Hobby Club.
  3. If someone says "stop" or goes limp, taps out, the hobby is over.
  4. Only two hobby enthusiasts to a hobby.
  5. One hobby at a time.
  6. No shirts, no shoes.
  7. Hobbies will go on as long as they have to.
  8. If this is your first night at Hobby Club, you have to hobby.
